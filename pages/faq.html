General Questions

What is ScholarQuest?

+

ScholarQuest is a platform that connects students with scholarship opportunities. We verify and curate scholarships to ensure they are legitimate and match them with eligible students.

Is ScholarQuest free to use?

+

Yes! ScholarQuest is completely free for students. We believe in making education accessible to everyone.

Application Process

How do I apply for scholarships?

+

Create an account, complete your profile, and our matching system will show you relevant scholarships. Click "Apply" to start your application process.
